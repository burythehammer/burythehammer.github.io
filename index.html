hello!

I'm Matt Long.

I am a software engineer and a senior consultant for OpenCredo, an open source software consultancy based in London.

I worked as a QA consultant for many years, and built test automation frameworks in half a dozen programming languages, including Go, Java, Javascript, and more. After working on many cloud infrastructure projects, I have made the leap to becoming a platform engineer. Given these dual specialities, I am particularly interested in the intersection of software testing and modern cloud technology.

I have presented talks at QCon, TestBash, muCon, Agile Cambridge, and many smaller meetups across London.

I am a keen runner, an indiepop fan, and quote The Simpsons too much. I live in Shoreditch with my partner and an unbearably cute British shorthair kitten.

If you're interested in hiring me, or getting me to do a talk, get in touch via LinkedIn.
