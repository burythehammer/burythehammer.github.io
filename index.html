hello!

I'm Matt Long.

I am a QA & devops consultant for OpenCredo, an open source software consultancy based in London.

I've worked as a QA consultant for many years, and built test automation frameworks in half a dozen programming languages. After working on many cloud infrastructure projects, I've become particularly interested in the intersection of modern cloud infrastructure and testing.

I have presented talks at QCon, TestBash, muCon, Agile Cambridge, and many smaller meetups across London.

In my spare time, I listen to indiepop music, play videogames, watch pretentious arthouse movies, and relentlessly quote The Simpsons. I also build and maintain a machine learning foosball bot!
